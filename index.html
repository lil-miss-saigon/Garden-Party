Survival Guide

Soil contains all the essential minerals that make plant life possible, the most important being Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K).

A bag of soil in your inventory displays the balance of these elements in its randomly generated (N)(P)(K) ratio.

Higher-grade soil yields a ratio within a range progressively closer to the ideal ratio of (50)(10)(150).

The closer a pot's (N)(P)(K) is to the ideal ratio, the higher its plant's health will be. Healthy plants generate more money than sickly ones, and are more likely to survive weeds, diseases, infestations, and bad weather. A plant's max revenue is the highest it can generate at peak health.

Fertilizer grants a slight bonus to a specific element. Get closer to the ideal ratio by choosing the right grade of fertilizer.

* Survive storms by waiting them out
* Survive infestations by locating and   killing the source
* Survive diseases by removing all   infected plants
* Watch out for weeds and remove them   as needed
